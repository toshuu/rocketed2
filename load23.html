Chapter - 2

Top 3 Dying Industries

With that said , we need to understand what's not worth spending time into ... So these are top dying industries. First , here are 3 that might matter the most to lot of masses.

A video disc.



Video Tape & Discs

Video Disc & Tapes are still medium for lot of people for money. But it would not be good for them to not change the path now. With Netflix & Other on-demand services and faster data transmission , people have migrated.

Collection of newspapers



Newspaper Publishers

Newspaper Industry saw a great fall in subscriber base since everything is moving digital. That is exactly why bigger players are moving with $1 subscription plans over digital media. This scenario is a classic migration of offline to online usage of particular content. Bigger players like New York Times are moving quickly to offer wider services over digital medium.

A representation of photo finish.



Photofinishing Industry

This is very controversial point. But there is a prediction that photo finishing as an industry will soon fall down. A lot of tools have been coming with simpler and automatic editing features that makes your photos look good. That's a potential threat to pro photo editors over longer period. However, one might doubt this one and argue that there might still be need of pros.

Chapter - 3

5 More Dying Industries

The industries are important because lots of people rely on them. Let us look at more industries that might not last long.

A Library



Library Archives

Library is a good place for one to read books. However , with companies like Amazon , which started as book sellers & then companies like Blinkist , which started offering digital summaries over smartphone so that you save time , the libraries are facing the fall of customer base over time.

A directory



Directory / Mailing List Publications

Even though , in the past it has helped a lot for lot of things, the directories are now the thing of past. The internet age is just one click away from literally every and any information with more and more accuracy. The data collection has moved to online world over offline.

Telephone Equipment



Telephone Manufacturing Appratus

The telephone is still used in commercial offices and workplaces but the apparatus making industries have seen a drastic decrease in business , as predicted multiple times. The rise in wireless and modern mobile telecommunication is killing (not killed) the industry of telephones.

A sound studio



Sound Recording Studios

The professionals in sound industries are making a bold bet that sound recording studio industry will not be very good idea in coming future. We will try to cover this separately. But it remains a question if this is a good prediction or a bad one.

A bookstore



Book & Newspaper Dealer

Bookstore & Newspaper dealers has been in problem ever since mobile technology took over. Now that everything has been digitized , even the content of the newspaper & books content... It is hard for them to sustain.

Top 12 Growing Industries - Set B

Let us look at another set of growing industries , Next 4 are related with content, people and the joy.

Digital Content Marketing



Content Marketing

The content marketing is an industry in itself in modern time. Gone are the days , where direct selling was possible. Now you need to Educate , Engage and then Sell to customers. However , Content Marketing can sell anything over to the customers since the center is the content that people are consuming which may vary on lot of different subjects & topics.



Beauty Tech

Beauty Tech covers a lot of ranges of product which are designed to fulfill primary objective , that is to make people look good. Everything from Cleansers to Cosmetics and Fashion Accessories are part of this and these are growing at an incredible rate of growth. Top 12 Growing Industries - Set C

Now that we have covered 8 industries that are growing , Let's look at last 4 .. Set C consists of industries related to Life,Money & Security.



BioTech

Medical Drugs and other Bio products being the highlight. BioTech is industry that develop technologies that help improve our lives and the health of our planet. This was and is and predicted to same , that is towards growth industry.

An old age representation.



Elder Care

Elder care will be big industries due to the fact that user base of such services will be large enough. There are newer entities that promises fulfillment of the special needs and requirements that are unique to senior citizens. Services includes assisted living, adult day care, long term care, nursing homes, hospice care, and home care.

Cyber Security representation



Cyber Security

Cyber security or information technology security are the techniques of protecting computers, networks, programs and data from unauthorized access or attacks that are aimed for exploitation. With growing data & internet companies, it is an absolute necessity to keep users data safe and it is predicted to be in long term period.

Financial Technology



Fin Tech

At its core, fintech is utilized to help companies, business owners and consumers better manage their financial operations, processes, and lives by utilizing specialized software and algorithms that are used on computers and, increasingly, smartphones. The growing number of businesses ,consumers over various sectors & companies means that this industry might witness a major growth in coming years.
